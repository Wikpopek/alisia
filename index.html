Mysterious whale in the lake!

This lake is located in the south of Italy. Recently, for some unknown reason, a whale appeared there and it was a great attraction for all the residents and people who love traveling. Alisia decided to check it out and go on a trip in her new car. Italy is one of her favorite countries to travel to, so she didn't hesitate for long and quickly decided to do some research on how to best get there.
